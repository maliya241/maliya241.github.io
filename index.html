About Me

Hello! I am Maria McConkey, and I love coding and creating. Creating has been in my life since I was little. From school assignments to independent projects, I have found myself wanting to push the envelope. My artwork allows me to think completely outside the box. My writing demontrates my outside the box thinking while staying inside the box. Coding is both of those things. It is challenging. It is problem solving. It is dreaming. It is something I love to do. I got my feet wet with watching beginner coding videos and completing drag-and-drop code projects. I dove in deeper when I took a coding class at school. Now I want to keep swimming in the open ocean of computer science.

Projects

Socktober

http://happysocktober.com/

Socktober is a movement during the month of October to encourage people to collect and donate socks and other necessities to homeless shelters. It has become an international social movement. I was one of four selected to help build a website for this movement. I mainly took care of the graphics for the website. I created the logo, flyers, and many of the other handdrawn images found on the website.

https://github.com/maliya241/socktober

TeenHub

Not Live

TeenHub was built to create more involvement for my community. There are several teenagers joining gangs in my hometown because they find friends and family in those gangs. If there were more community events for teenagers to attend, then those lonely teens may find relationships outside those gangs. There actually may be several things for teenagers to be a part of; however, I do not know about them, and so most likely others do not either. I built this website as a solution to help teens find events in the Jackson, TN area.

https://github.com/maliya241/TeenHub

American History Project

This website about the Battle of Fredericksburg was built for an American History project. For the American History project, I was allowed to present information about a Civil War battle any way I wanted to so I decided to test my coding skills and build a website. It is a simple website, but it is the first independent website I have built.

https://github.com/maliya241/AHProject
